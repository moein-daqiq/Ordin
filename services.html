What do we mean by “Services”?

Services are the hands-on help around the platform — getting you live, making workflows simple, and ensuring payroll, HR, and contractor processes stay consistent and audit-ready.

What ORDIN Services include

Implementation & setup

Configure your ORDIN workspace, structure roles, set up approvals, and organise your people and contractor flows.

Workflow design

Turn your current messy process into a clean, repeatable system — onboarding, payroll inputs, documents, and approvals.

Compliance support

Help aligning processes with UK expectations (HMRC-facing workflows, record keeping, and audit trail readiness).

Data migration

Bring in your employee/contractor records from spreadsheets or existing tools with minimal disruption.

Training & enablement

Train admins and managers so approvals, documents, and payroll inputs run smoothly from day one.

Ongoing support

Help you keep things running: questions, changes, troubleshooting, and improving workflows over time.
