The ORDIN platform

ORDIN is your UK-first employment infrastructure — a single system to manage people, contracts, approvals, documents, and an audit trail that stays consistent across Payroll, HR, IT, and Services.

Contracts Approvals Documents Audit trail Workflows UK-first compliance

What is the ORDIN platform?

The platform is the shared foundation that makes everything else reliable: one source of truth for worker records, contract terms, approvals, and changes over time. Instead of scattered emails, spreadsheets, and tools, ORDIN keeps the operational history in one place.

When payroll, HR, and IT live in separate systems, the same employee exists in three different versions. ORDIN solves that by connecting your workflows and maintaining a consistent audit trail.

Core building blocks

People directory

One profile per worker with role, start date, contract terms, documents, and operational history.

Contracts & templates

Generate offers and contracts from templates, track versions, and keep changes structured and traceable.

Approvals workflow

Route key changes through the right decision makers: salary updates, role changes, access requests, and policy acknowledgements.

Documents & storage

Store and organise critical files: contracts, policies, and supporting documents — with controlled access.

Audit trail by default

Every action is logged: who did what, when, and why — designed for accountability and compliance.

UK-first compliance posture

Workflows designed around UK expectations: consistent records, clear approvals, and HMRC-facing readiness.

How the platform connects Payroll, HR, IT, and Services

Payroll
Uses contract terms, approved changes, and accurate people data to run payroll with fewer errors and stronger traceability.
HR (HRIS)
Policies, acknowledgements, leave, and documentation live on the same worker record — reducing disputes and confusion.
IT
Onboarding/offboarding workflows trigger from HR events, and access/device actions are recorded in the audit trail.
Services
Implementation and advisory services configure the platform to match your real workflows — so the system sticks.

Why a single platform matters

When systems are disconnected, businesses rely on manual checks, repeated data entry, and memory. That’s where errors, delays, and compliance gaps happen. A single platform creates repeatable operations with consistent records and a clear audit trail.

What happens without a platform foundation?

Duplicate data & mistakes

Employee details differ across tools. Payroll errors increase, and teams waste time fixing issues.

Weak accountability

Approvals happen in messages and calls, with no traceable record of who approved what.

Audit pain

When something goes wrong, the evidence is scattered — slow to collect and hard to prove.

ORDIN is infrastructure, not just software

ORDIN is designed to be the operating layer underneath employment workflows — built to help UK teams run faster with less risk. Payroll, HR, IT and services all rely on the same foundation: people data, contracts, approvals and an audit trail.
